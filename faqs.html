Loan Information

Depending on the type and duration of loan, our interest rates are from 4% per month for monthly/weekly loans, and 10% for daily loans with 20-day duration.

A one-time administrative fee applies at the point of disbursement. Also, there may be charges for late repayment, however this will be clearly spelt out in every loan contract and explained to you before you execute it.

Application Process
