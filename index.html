Who We Are

Prospera Credit is a forward-thinking lending company based in Nigeria, dedicated to driving financial inclusion and economic empowerment.

We specialize in providing accessible and affordable credit solutions to low-income individuals, rural communities, micro and small enterprises (MSMEs), and women-led businesses.
