Frequently Asked Questions

How do I apply for a loan?

You can apply for a loan by visiting any of our branches, filling out the online application form on our website, or contacting our customer service team.

How long does it take to get a loan?

Once all required documents are submitted, you can receive your loan within 1-3 business days.

What documents do I need to apply?

You'll need a valid ID, proof of business, and bank statements (where applicable). A guarantor is also required.
